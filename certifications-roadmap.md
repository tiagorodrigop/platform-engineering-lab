# Cloud Architecture Foundations

## AWS Well Architected Framework

Six pillars:

### Operational Excellence
Automate deployments and operations, continuously improve processes.

### Security
Protect systems, services, and data with strong identity, encryption, and monitoring.

### Reliability
Ensure the system can recover from failures and maintain availability.

### Performance Efficiency
Use cloud resources efficiently and scale as demand changes.

### Cost Optimization
Avoid unnecessary costs and continuously optimize resource usage.

### Sustainability
Reduce environmental impact by optimizing workloads and infrastructure.
